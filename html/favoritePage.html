Great White

The Great White Shark is an incredible animal. They grow up to 6,600 pounds and 20 feet long! Great whites have very cool hunting tactics. To hunt seals, they lurk in the depths of the ocean until they see a seal above them. They then swim as fast as they can towards the surface. Once at the surface, they breach the water and soar through the air with the seal in their mouth. The seal never knew what hit them!
